Goal:
Make the Marketing area dropdown and the Affirm Me button clearly visible over the floral hero, with a refined white‑outline liquid-glass look. The dropdown must look like a dropdown, open directly under the trigger, be keyboard accessible, and not visually fight the hero.

0) House rules

Don’t touch the Buy Me a Coffee button.

Keep serif for headings/quotes; use system UI sans (600) for controls.

Glass effects must use rgba(...) backgrounds + backdrop-filter. No parent with opacity:<1 or filter on the controls container (that flattens blur).

1) Controls tray (visibility anchor for the dropdown + CTA)

Create a small glass tray wrapper behind the dropdown trigger and the “Affirm Me” button (not full‑width).

position: relative; display: inline-flex; gap: 10px; align-items: center;

Padding: 6px 12px; Radius: 999px

background: rgba(0,0,0,.12);

backdrop-filter: blur(20px) saturate(130%);

-webkit-backdrop-filter: blur(20px) saturate(130%);

Shadow: 0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,.35);

No border.
This gives the controls a subtle base so they don’t vanish into petals.

2) Outline styles (trigger + CTA)

Apply a shared outline recipe:

Font: system-ui, -apple-system, Segoe UI, Roboto, Inter, "Helvetica Neue", Arial, sans-serif; 600

Height: 42–44px (CTA), 40–42px (dropdown trigger)

Padding: CTA 0 18px; Trigger 0 16px (plus room for chevron)

Radius: 999px

Border: 1.5px solid rgba(255,255,255,.80) (hover to .95)

Fill: default transparent; on hover add 6–8% white wash (rgba(255,255,255,.08))

Text: #FFFFFFEE

Motion:

Hover: translateY(-1px); 140ms cubic-bezier(.2,.8,.2,1)

Active: transform: translateY(0) scale(.985); 90ms

Focus: inner 1px white + outer 3–4px soft gold (#CBA86266)

Respect prefers-reduced-motion: disable translate/scale; keep color/border changes only.

3) Dropdown trigger (the pill you click)

Add a chevron element inside the trigger on the right:

Size ~ 12px; opacity:.9

Rotate 0° → 180° when open (120ms)

Increase right padding to clear the chevron (about +20–24px).

4) Dropdown menu (matte glass panel, readable)

Menu must open directly below the trigger, left‑aligned to the trigger’s left edge.

Positioning & alignment

Ensure the trigger’s wrapper is position: relative.

Menu: position: absolute; top: calc(100% + 8px); left: 0;

z-index: 90 so it sits above the card edge.

Prevent it from centering over the page (current bug): tie it to the trigger, not the viewport.

Panel visual

Background: rgba(16,18,20,.28) (dark matte glass)

backdrop-filter: blur(10px) saturate(120%)

Border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,.14)

Radius: 14–16px

Shadow: 0 14px 36px rgba(0,0,0,.45)

Max-height: 280px with vertical scroll

Scrollbar: thin, low‑contrast; don’t show until needed

Menu items

Each option: padding 10–12px 14px; radius 10px

Text color: #FBF8F3

Hover: background rgba(255,255,255,.06)

Selected: add a small gold dot • before the label (no full highlight)

Touch target ≥ 40px height

Open/close animation

Menu: opacity 120ms ease-out (0→1) + transform: translateY(6px→0)

On close: reverse

Chevron rotates with the same timing

5) Accessibility (must-haves)

Trigger: aria-haspopup="listbox" and aria-expanded="true/false"

Menu: role="listbox"; items: role="option" with aria-selected="true" on the current one

Keyboard:

Enter/Space: open/close

Esc: close and return focus to trigger

Up/Down: move active option

Home/End: jump to first/last

Close on outside click and on focus leaving the menu.

Do not trap focus globally; keep focus within trigger + menu while open.

6) Card buttons (“Share”, “Copy”) consistency

Use the light version of the outline/glass to remain secondary on the card:

Border: 1px soft gold rgba(203,168,98,.40) (hover .60)

Fill: rgba(255,255,255,.22) + backdrop-filter: blur(10px) saturate(120%)

Text: deep green #17382F

Height: 38–40px; Padding 0 16px

Hover: translateY(-1px) + brightness +3%

Focus: same white+gold ring

Keep a 12–16px gap between the two; ensure container doesn’t clip focus rings

7) Acceptance criteria

The dropdown opens under its trigger, not centered on the page; chevron rotates; menu is readable over florals.

The Affirm Me button and dropdown are clearly visible at a glance thanks to the tray + thicker outline + subtle fill on hover.

Share/Copy look related but visibly secondary on the light card.

All controls have consistent motion, focus rings, and respect prefers-reduced-motion.
